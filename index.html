Hello there! I'm Abhir. I'm a recent graduate of the University of Virginia School of Engineering (UVa) , where I obtained my Bachelor's in Computer Science . My studies, work, and interests in the areas of computing and electronics are driven by my desire to enable machines to seamlessly collaborate with us humans.

My current research with FloodWatch revolves around machine learning and digital signal processing. Some areas that I am currently exploring are multimodal fusions for water level monitoring, and developing a verification framework for multi-stream sensor anomaly detection.

I have been doing work in robotics and autonomous vehicle control. I plan to pursue research in curating generalizable robotic action, interactive perception, and robust manipulative robotics as I am quite fascinated in translating the sensorimotor principles and proprioception that we humans have to robots and applying it to manipulative tasks in situations where relevant information is not known apriori.
